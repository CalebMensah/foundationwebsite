About Us

Learn about our mission, vision, and the people behind Horizon Foundation.

Our Mission

To empower communities through sustainable development initiatives that promote education, healthcare, and environmental conservation.

Our Vision

A world where all communities have access to quality education, healthcare, and a clean environment, enabling them to thrive and prosper.

Our Story

Horizon Foundation was established in 2010 by a group of passionate individuals with a shared vision of creating lasting positive change in the world. What began as a small initiative in a single community has grown into an international organization with projects spanning across 20+ countries.

Our founder, Dr. Sarah Johnson, witnessed firsthand the transformative power of education while volunteering in rural communities. This experience inspired her to create a foundation that would address not only educational needs but also healthcare and environmental challenges that communities face.

Over the past decade, we have collaborated with local organizations, governments, and communities to implement sustainable solutions that empower people to build better futures for themselves and generations to come.
