Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate to the Horizon Foundation?

You can donate through our website by visiting the Donate page, by phone at +1 (555) 765-4321, or by mail to our main office address. We accept various payment methods including credit cards, bank transfers, and checks.

How can I volunteer with your organization?

We welcome volunteers in various capacities. Please fill out our contact form and select "Volunteering" as the subject, or email us directly at volunteers@horizonfoundation.org. We'll connect you with opportunities that match your skills and interests.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, the Horizon Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, and all donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt for your donation for tax purposes.

How can my company partner with Horizon Foundation?

We offer various partnership opportunities for companies, including corporate sponsorships, cause marketing, employee engagement programs, and in-kind donations. Please contact our partnerships team at partners@horizonfoundation.org to discuss collaboration possibilities.
