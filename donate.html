Frequently Asked Questions

How is my donation used?

90% of all donations go directly to our programs in the field. The remaining 10% is used for administration, fundraising, and operational costs to ensure we can continue our mission effectively. We publish detailed financial reports annually that show exactly how funds are allocated.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Horizon Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations made by US taxpayers are tax-deductible to the full extent allowed by law. You will receive a tax receipt via email immediately after your donation.

Can I make a donation in honor or memory of someone?

Absolutely! When completing the donation form, you'll have the option to dedicate your gift in honor or memory of someone special. We can also send a notification to that person or their family about your thoughtful gift.

How can I change or cancel my monthly donation?

You can manage your recurring donations at any time by logging into your donor account on our website. Alternatively, you can contact our donor support team at support@horizonfoundation.org or call us at (800) 555-1234.

Can I donate if I live outside the United States?

Yes, we accept donations from around the world. However, tax benefits may vary depending on your country's laws. For specific information about tax benefits in your country, please consult with a local tax advisor.

Have more questions? Contact our donor support team.
